Legolas Athletic Program

All entering college freshmen must meet the applicable NCAA initial academic eligibility requirements in order to participate in athletics at GRINDELWALD University. All entering freshmen must have graduated from high school, successfully completed a core curriculum, and taken a national standardized test. Your core curriculum GPA and standardized test scores will be reviewed and applied to the NCAA initial eligibility index. This will identify your initial eligibility status and determine if, and to what extent, you are able to participate in intercollegiate athletics as an entering freshman.
